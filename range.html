In an attempt to reduce a crazy one million single use cafe cups per year in Australia, our reuse mugs are made from bamboo fibre - an eco-friendly, sustainable material.

Light weight and durable, our mugs have no rubbery/plastic taste. 350ml (120z size) and dishwasher safe. A screw to and supper plug secures a spell-free seal.

Currently we have 7 different designs in our range - 4 graphic styles and 3 'bush' series designs

Custom designs for cafes / businesses / promos are possible with a minimum of 500pcs. Contact us for more information about a bespoke mug
